# neoteric-roots-360
Virtual Monocultures / Invisible Nets is a 360° environment built from AI-generated landscapes — synthetic vineyards grown through iterative text-to-image prompts on a model trained on photographs, suspended between photographic reference, algorithmic reconstruction, and simulation. Transparent wireframe structures drift across the terrain like data silos, virtual architectures, and invisible infrastructures. The decades-old vines stand for rootedness, community, and continuity, while the monoculture of viticulture echoes the standardization and optimization now unfolding in virtual space — the digital systems, platforms, and AI models that increasingly structure perception, communication, and identity. Neither utopia nor dystopia, the work makes these invisible nets visible and asks what diversity, memory, and identity persist in an algorithmically shaped world — and what new forms of connection and cultural continuity might emerge from it.
